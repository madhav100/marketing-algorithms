Template for exploring customer revenue relationships across the five customer CSV sources.

SELECT customerId, SUM(netRevenue) - SUM(refundAmount) AS realizedRevenue
FROM customer_orders LEFT JOIN customer_returns USING (customerId)
GROUP BY customerId;
