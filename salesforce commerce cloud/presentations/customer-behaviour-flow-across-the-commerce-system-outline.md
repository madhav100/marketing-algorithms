# Customer Behaviour Flow Across the Commerce System

## Slide 1 — Title & Objective
- Title: Customer Behaviour Flow Across the Commerce System
- Audience: Technical business managers and client stakeholders
- Goal: Explain how storefront, admin console, and data cloud operate as one client-focused flow

## Slide 2 — Executive Summary
- Client-first architecture captures behaviour once and reuses it across domains
- Storefront drives engagement, Admin Console drives control, Data Cloud drives intelligence
- Closed loop enables continuous optimization

## Slide 3 — System Landscape
- Storefront (Walmart): discovery, cart, checkout, interaction events
- Admin Console: catalog, pricing, promotions, operations
- Data Cloud: identity stitching, segmentation, activation, measurement

## Slide 4 — Storefront Layer
- Captures behavioural signals: views, search, add-to-cart, checkout, drop-off
- Supports near real-time customer journey visibility
- Client impact: UX improvement and conversion optimization

## Slide 5 — Admin Console Layer
- Converts business strategy into storefront execution
- Manages merchandising, offers, compliance and release control
- Client impact: controlled experiments and operational accountability

## Slide 6 — Data Cloud Layer
- Unifies behavioural telemetry with customer and commerce data
- Produces segments, propensity signals, and performance cohorts
- Feeds intelligence back to operations and reporting

## Slide 7 — End-to-End Behaviour Flow
- Customer activity begins in Storefront
- Admin-configured rules determine what customers see
- Data Cloud enriches and analyzes behaviour
- Insights return to teams for the next optimization cycle

## Slide 8 — Client-Centric Outcomes
- Revenue outcomes: conversion lift, basket growth, campaign efficiency
- Experience outcomes: relevance, lower friction, faster anomaly response
- Governance outcomes: KPI visibility and repeatable optimization

## Slide 9 — Key Success Factors
- Event taxonomy and tracking quality discipline
- Tight alignment between business operators and engineering teams
- Weekly feedback cadence from data to admin action
- Shared client scorecard: traffic quality, conversion, retention, margin

## Slide 10 — Roadmap & Next Steps
- Phase 1: tracking and governance baseline
- Phase 2: expanded segmentation and orchestration
- Phase 3: scale playbooks across categories and regions
- Immediate ask: approve KPI set and pilot cohort
